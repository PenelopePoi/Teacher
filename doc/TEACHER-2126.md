*Full document preserved in the Teacher repository as a design north star. The commitments that survive a century are commitments about the person, not the technology.*

- 2.4-second breathing glow matches resting human respiration
- Amber claims attention because of the human visual cortex
- Mint reads as calm because the nervous system evolved against chlorophyll
- One indicator carries all state because the attention budget is finite
- No spinners because indeterminate waits are worse than paced ones

*These were not design decisions contingent on 2026 hardware. They were design decisions contingent on the species the tool was built for.*
