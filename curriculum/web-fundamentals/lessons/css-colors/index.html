Our Story

Sweet Roots started in a home kitchen with a family recipe for tres leches cake. Three years later, we serve the whole neighborhood. Every loaf is made from scratch, every morning before sunrise.

Today's Menu

  • Tres Leches Cake — our signature
  • Sourdough Boule — 24-hour ferment
  • Guava Pastelitos — flaky and sweet
  • Cafe con Leche — strong and smooth
